## Scoring Criteria Overview

The CCMF Vetting Team evaluates applications using the following weighted criteria (total: 100%):

| Criterion | Weight | Key Evaluation Factors |
|----------|--------|------------------------|
| **Project Management Team** | **30%** | Prior track record, execution ability, probability of launch, individual qualifications, team job allocation |
| **Business Model & Time to Market** | **30%** | Vision/direction, market need, target market, marketing strategy, project viability, **3-month launch encouraged** |
| **Creativity & Innovation** | **30%** | Innovative technologies, creative solutions, disruptive capability, emerging/breakthrough technologies |
| **Social Responsibility** | **10%** | Open source contribution, ethical decision-making, solving social problems |

**Source:** `Docs/CCMF-GUIDES-NOTES-REV7.md` Section 4.1 (Vetting Criteria and Weighting)

## Strategic Application Tips

### 1. Maximize Section 2.3 (Self-Introduction) - 30% Team Score
- ‚úÖ Use **all 150 words (EN)** and **100 words (CH)** - every word counts
- ‚úÖ Include **specific metrics**: "Shipped 3 apps with 10K+ downloads"
- ‚úÖ Mention **GitHub profile** or **project links** (if space allows)
- ‚úÖ Show **execution proof**: "Led team of 3, delivered MVP in 6 weeks"
- ‚úÖ Highlight **relevant qualifications**: "5+ years Unity/AR development"

### 2. Maximize Section 2.4b (Project Description) - 70% Total Score Impact!
This single section contributes to **3 out of 4 criteria**:
- Business Model (30%)
- Innovation (30%)
- Social Responsibility (10%)

**Strategy:**
- ‚úÖ **Problem** (1-2 sentences): Clear, data-backed problem statement
- ‚úÖ **Solution** (2-3 sentences): Innovative approach with key technologies
- ‚úÖ **Innovation Highlights** (2-3 sentences): Specific tech stack, performance metrics
- ‚úÖ **Target Market** (1 sentence): Specific, measurable segments
- ‚úÖ **Social Impact** (1-2 sentences): Who benefits, measurable outcomes

**Word Allocation Example (150 words):**
- Problem: 20 words
- Solution: 40 words
- Innovation: 40 words
- Market: 20 words
- Social Impact: 30 words

### 3. Strengthen with Section 2.9 (Supplementary Information)
Use this optional section to:
- ‚úÖ Provide **3-month launch timeline** (addresses Time to Market)
- ‚úÖ Detail **open source contributions** (addresses Social Responsibility)
- ‚úÖ Explain **technical innovation** in depth (addresses Innovation)
- ‚úÖ Share **market validation data** (addresses Business Model)

### 4. Provide Evidence via Section 4.3 (Supplementary Documents)
Upload:
- ‚úÖ **CV/Resume** - Shows qualifications and track record (Team 30%)
- ‚úÖ **Team Structure Chart** - Visual organization (Team 30%)
- ‚úÖ **Portfolio/GitHub Links** - Execution proof (Team 30%, Innovation 30%)
- ‚úÖ **Demo Video Links** - Innovation demonstration (Innovation 30%)

### 5. Address Time to Market (Business Model 30%)
**Key Requirement:** "Product launch within 3 months from grant disbursement will be encouraged"

**How to Address:**
- Mention launch timeline in Section 2.4b (Idea Description)
- Use Section 2.9 to provide detailed month-by-month plan
- Show realistic 3-month beta launch plan

### 6. Word Count Strategy
**Strict Limits:**
- Self-Introduction EN: **150 words** (exactly)
- Self-Introduction CH: **100 words** (exactly)
- Project Description EN: **150 words** (exactly)
- Project Description CH: **150 words** (exactly)

**Tips:**
- ‚úÖ Count words carefully before pasting
- ‚úÖ Use word count tool (Microsoft Word, Google Docs)
- ‚úÖ Leave 2-3 word buffer for final edits
- ‚úÖ Prioritize high-impact content

### 7. Language Strategy
- **English:** Primary language, required for all English fields
- **Chinese:** Optional but recommended for GBAYEP (Greater Bay Area focus)
- **Consistency:** Use same Chinese variant throughout (Traditional OR Simplified)
