## Section 1: Profile and Eligibility

### 1.1 Age Requirement
**Question:** Are you aged between 18 and 30 years old as of the application deadline on December 5, 2025?

**Options:**
- Yes
- No

**Requirements:**
- Applicants must be between **18-30 inclusive**
- Born between **December 6, 1994** and **December 5, 2007**

**Note:** This is a strict requirement. If you do not meet this age requirement, you may need to apply for the CCMF Hong Kong Programme instead.

---

### 1.2 Residency Requirement
**Question:** Are you currently residing in Hong Kong, Macau or in Guangdong Province?

**Options:**
- Yes
- No

**Requirements:**
- Must currently reside in one of these regions:
  - Hong Kong
  - Macau
  - Guangdong Province (Mainland China)

---

### 1.3 Education Requirement
**Question:** Are you currently enrolled in or have graduated from a registered post-secondary education institution in Hong Kong, Macau or Guangdong no more than 3 years as of the application deadline on December 5, 2025?

**Options:**
- Yes
- No

**Requirements:**
- Must be enrolled OR graduated from a registered post-secondary institution in:
  - Hong Kong, OR
  - Macau, OR
  - Guangdong Province
- **Graduation/Enrollment Date:** Must be enrolled or graduated **on or after December 6, 2021**
- **Time Window:** Within 3 years of application deadline (December 5, 2025)

**Calculation:**
- Application deadline: December 5, 2025
- 3 years back: December 6, 2022
- Must have enrolled/graduated: December 6, 2021 or later

---
